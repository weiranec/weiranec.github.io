about

I am currently a Research Fellow in the department of applied biology and chemical technology of the Hong Kong Polytechnic University, with Dr. Lawrence Yoon Suk Lee and Prof. Dr. K. Y. Wong.

My main interest is in-situ electrochemistry and electrocatalysis using p/d-block elements, including but not limited to water electrolysis and glucose electrooxidation.
For specific topics, please refer to my research.
