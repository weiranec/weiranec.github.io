About me

I am currently a Research Fellow in the department of applied biology and chemical technology (ABCT) of the Hong Kong Polytechnic University (PolyU), with Dr. Lawrence Yoon Suk Lee and Prof. Dr. K. Y. Wong as the group leaders.

My main interest is in-situ electrochemistry and electrocatalysis using p/d-block elements, including but not limited to water electrolysis and glucose electrooxidation. My personal research goal include: (1) to understand electrocatalytic interface at atomic level; and (2) to rational design electrocatalyst with 100% (and beyond) atomic efficency.

I am a member of the International Society of Electrochemistry (ISE), also a regular reviewer for academic journals, such as Communications Chemistry and Sensors & Actuators, B: Chemical.
